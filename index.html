Welcome to

Schwarzsee

The Schwarzsee is romantically nestled in the Fribourg Pre-Alps and, like the village, is part of the German-speaking Sense district. Be inspired by the variety of experiences in Schwarzsee. We would be delighted to take you on a journey of discovery in our beautiful region.
