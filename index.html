This is the Schwarzsee

Nestled romantically in the Fribourg Pre-Alps, Schwarzsee and its charming village belong to the German-speaking Sense district. Explore the Beauty of Schwarzsee

The first glimpse of Schwarzsee will leave you in awe — from the striking whale fin rising from the water to the impressive dragon sculpture along the lakeside trail. On sunny days, the lake invites you to swim, fish, paddleboard, or enjoy a relaxing pedalo ride. During winter, when the lake freezes over, it transforms into a natural ice rink perfect for skating. The surrounding region offers scenic hiking trails that wind through flower-filled meadows and rugged mountainsides. In the colder months, you can explore the peaceful, snow-covered landscape on snowshoes.

Riggisalp

Take the chair lift from Schwarzsee up to Riggisalp, just below the Schafberg. Visit a traditional alpine dairy and see how cheese is made over an open fire. Set out on a hike or bike ride to the Euschelspass, head toward the Kaiseregg, or explore the untamed beauty of the Brecca Valley. Treat yourself to delicious local products from an alpine farm, then race back down to the valley on a monster scooter — or, in winter, enjoy a thrilling sled ride from Riggisalp.

Around Schwarzsee

Join a guided adventure through the stunning Gantrisch Nature Park and feel the breeze on the “Heart Loop” — an exciting e-bike route through the Senseland. In Charmey, unwind at the relaxing Bains de la Gruyère wellness spa. Don’t miss Jaun, famous for its beautiful waterfall, and the charming town of Gruyères, home to its iconic castle and nearby nature park — both are must-see destinations.

Things to Do Around Schwarzsee

Hike to the Brecca Valley

Explore one of the most beautiful and wild valleys in the Swiss Pre-Alps. This glacial valley offers scenic trails, alpine meadows, and local wildlife — perfect for hikers and photographers.

Visit a Traditional Alpine Cheese Dairy

Watch local cheesemakers craft fresh alpine cheese over an open fire — a truly authentic Swiss experience! You can also taste and buy handmade cheese directly from the dairy.

Take the Chairlift to Riggisalp

Ride up to Riggisalp for stunning views of the Schwarzsee region and the surrounding peaks. It’s a great starting point for hikes, bike rides, or simply relaxing at the mountain restaurant.

Boating or Swimming in Schwarzsee

Enjoy the crystal-clear mountain lake by renting a rowboat, paddleboat, or taking a refreshing dip during summer. The calm atmosphere makes it ideal for relaxation and picnics.

Hike to Euschelspass or Kaiseregg

For more adventurous visitors, hiking trails lead toward the Euschelspass or up to the Kaiseregg summit — offering breathtaking panoramas of the Swiss Alps.

Winter Fun – Sledding & Snowshoeing

In winter, the area turns into a snowy playground. Go sledding from Riggisalp, try snowshoe trails, or simply enjoy the peaceful snowy scenery around the frozen lake.
